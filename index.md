I started my education in IT in September 2019 at the age of 28, transitioning from the paper-based world of lawyering. Up until then my realm was the Microsoft Office suite and email clients. [Graham Lee](https://twitter.com/iwasleeg) says that Excel is the most popular IDE in the world. By that token, I had some measure of experience with an IDE. Apart from that, I had never done programming as such. More importantly, I lacked the basic reference points to orient myself in the IT space. With this series of posts, I would like to provide a general compass to new recruits of the IT world. In essence, it is my two cents on software development and what I believe is useful to know to start developing code-based products.
